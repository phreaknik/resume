Career Profile

I am an electrical engineer with a passion for all things electronic. Since childhood, I have spent most of my time either tearing apart old gadgets or hacking together new creations of my own. This passion for tech exploration and innovation has had a strong impact on my career path.

I studied electrical engineering at Texas A&M, where I graduated with a Masters Degree in electrical engineering. In school I focused on analog and mixed signal VLSI design, and ultimately spent a few years doing semiconductor design. Throughout my time in school and at work in the semiconductor industry, I continued to work on personal projects spanning a wide array of technologies, ranging from embedded system design to machine learning mathematics.

After work, I would often “moonlight” as an independent contractor, helping startups prototype their tech ideas and in some cases bring those ideas to production. Now as CTO at Knocki, I get to do this every day! I work with a great team of engineers and developers to take wild new ideas off the whiteboard and onto the production line.
